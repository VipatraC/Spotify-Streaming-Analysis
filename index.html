Credit Risk Modeling & Simulation

This project builds a credit rating model inspired by how Moody's and S&P assess company risk. Using 3 years of financial statement data (2015–2017), it scores companies across 5 key metrics and assigns ratings from A to CCC — then uses Monte Carlo simulation to estimate how likely each rating group is to default.
